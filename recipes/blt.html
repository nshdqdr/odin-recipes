Description

This is just a basic Bacon Lettuce and Tomato sandwich. Hence the name 'BLT'. It can't get any simpler than this. If you're looking up a recipe for a BLT, something is really wrong with you!

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Bacon
  • Lettuce
  • Tomatoes
  • Bread

Steps

  1. Place a slice of bread
  2. Then some sliced tomatoes
  3. Then some lettuce
  4. And then the bacon
  5. Finally place another slice of bread on top
